PROSTHETIC HAND

Created in 2016 for my course titled ENG1C03: Engineering Design and Graphics, the goal was to create a prosthetic hand that was able to pinch with two "fingers". The actual pinching mechanism was to work using only a gear system with a specific gear ratio. The device was later built using 3D printers at the McMaster EPIC lab.
You can view a 3D model of the design by clicking the image on the left! Adobe Acrobat Reader is required to view the 3D pdf.


OVERDRIVE OST

Started work in 2011 when I was 14 years old, Overdrive was a videogame inspired by the likes of Cave Story and Spelunky, which never left the conceptual stages. What was completed included some rough pixel art and a complete original soundtrack. Although the concept has since been put aside, I do plan on one day revisiting the idea of creating an adventurous 2D platformer.
